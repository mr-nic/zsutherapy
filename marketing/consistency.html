Tone of Voice
Across Every Channel, Prefer
Calm — unhurried, no urgency pressure
Warm — personal, human, not corporate
Specific — real details over vague wellness language
Honest — no overclaiming, no hype
Reassuring — especially for first-time and pregnancy clients
Avoid Across Every Channel
Jargon or clinical language
Salesy or pushy phrasing
Wellness clichés (holistic journey, transformative experience)
Overclaiming (detoxing, cures, fixes)
Keyword-stuffed phrases that sound unnatural

The tone should feel like a very good practitioner who is warm, clear, and quietly expert

Quarterly Consistency Audit
Check These Four Things Every Quarter
Google Business Profile: name, address, hours, booking link, description — all current?
Instagram: bio, link, location tag, treatment names in captions — consistent?
Website: all 12 pages use the same treatment names and booking URL?
Fresha: treatments listed with same names and prices as website?
After Any Change (pricing, hours, new treatment)
Update Google Business Profile first — it has the widest reach
Update website second
Update Instagram bio and pinned posts third
Update Fresha last — confirm booking flow still works after any change

A change on one channel that is not reflected on others creates confusion and lost bookings
