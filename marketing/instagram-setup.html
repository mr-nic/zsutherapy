Phase 1 — Account Basics
Account Creation
Create a new account using a dedicated business email
Handle: @zsutherapy (already set)
Keep the handle simple and close to the real business name
Switch to Professional Account
Settings > Account > Switch to Professional Account
Select Business (gives booking button, contact details, and insights)
Category: Massage Therapist
Profile Photo
Use a warm, clear portrait of Zsu — not a logo
Same photo as the website About page for consistency
Good light, natural expression — it should feel like meeting someone

A face builds more trust than a brand mark for a sole practitioner

Phase 2 — Profile Setup
Name Field
Use: Zsu Therapy — Massage Brockley SE4
This is searchable on Instagram — location and service both help
Keep it close to the registered trading name
Bio — Calm Formula
Line 1: what you offer in plain language (e.g. Massage, reflexology, Thai yoga)
Line 2: where and how long (e.g. Brockley SE4 · since 2010)
Line 3: one reassuring line (e.g. One therapist. Always Zsu.)
Line 4: gentle CTA (e.g. Book via link below)
Avoid stacked hashtags or keyword-stuffed phrases in the bio

The bio should feel like an introduction, not a listing

Contact and Link
Add phone and WhatsApp number
Add physical address: Renaissance Yoga & Wellbeing, Mantle Road SE4 2EW
Link: zsutherapy.com/book-online (direct to booking, not homepage)
Add Booking Action Button: Settings > Edit Profile > Action Buttons
Phase 3 — Before Going Live
First 9 Posts — What Each Should Do
Post 1: Who Zsu is — warm introduction, face photo
Post 2: What deep tissue actually feels like — demystify
Post 3: The venue — Renaissance Yoga & Wellbeing, warm and calm
Post 4: Pregnancy massage — reassurance for first-timers
Post 5: Aromatherapy — oils, mood, seasonal
Post 6: Reflexology or Thai Yoga — explain the unfamiliar
Post 7: Testimonial or client outcome, carefully worded
Post 8: Pricing and session lengths — transparent, calm
Post 9: Available this week — first booking CTA

A grid of 9 before promoting means new visitors arrive at a profile that already feels established

Pinned Posts (3 pins)
Pin 1: Who Zsu is + full treatment list
Pin 2: Pricing and session lengths
Pin 3: Current availability + booking link
Phase 4 — Highlights
Set Up These 5 Highlight Albums
Treatments — one Story per treatment, what it is and who it helps
About — Zsu’s story, training, how she works
Reviews — shared client feedback (ask permission or keep anonymous)
FAQ — what to wear, what to expect, booking, cancellation
Book — availability, booking link, how to get in touch

Highlights are the first thing a new visitor explores after the bio
