Situation

What's happening right now

Zsu Therapy has strong foundations — 163 five-star Google reviews, a real domain with dedicated treatment pages, and 15+ years of trading history. The problem is almost entirely one of local keyword absence. Despite being based in Brockley SE4, the site doesn't mention Brockley, SE4, or South East London in any title tag or meta description. This means Google can't confidently associate the site with local searches, and the result is that newer, less-reviewed competitors rank above it for the most important terms.

There is also a complete absence from Apple Maps and Bing Places — making the site invisible on DuckDuckGo, Safari Maps, and Siri, which represents a significant share of mobile search. Bark.com is a further gap, though the value there is a free backlink rather than lead generation — at 15 years trading with 163 five-star reviews, Zsu is unlikely to need to pay for leads. Notably, WhoDoYou.com has automatically scraped multiple local Facebook community recommendations for Zsu going back to 2016 — organic goodwill that exists but isn't being amplified by structured directory presence elsewhere.

Priority 1

Quick wins — do this week

These six changes require no new content, no developer, and no budget. All can be done inside Wix's SEO settings or by claiming free platform listings.

Quick win

Rewrite every page title tag to include location

Title tags are the single most important on-page local ranking signal. Every page currently omits "Brockley", "SE4", and "South East London". Competitors who outrank Zsu on "massage Brockley SE4" have these words in their titles — that's the entire reason they rank above her.

In Wix: Settings → SEO → each page → Edit SEO Settings → Title tag field.

Add location to every meta description

Meta descriptions don't directly affect ranking but they do affect click-through rate — and Google often highlights the matching search term in bold. Including "Brockley", "SE4", and "South East London" means those words will bold in the search result when someone searches for local massage.

BEFORE — Homepage
–Welcome to Zsu Therapy. Experience personalised, therapeutic and relaxing massage and reflexology treatments in London, UK.
AFTER
+Personalised massage, reflexology & aromatherapy in Brockley SE4. Deep tissue, Thai yoga, pregnancy & hot stone. Est. 2010. 163 five-star reviews. Book online today.
Quick win

Claim Bing Places — 20 minutes, free

This is why Zsu doesn't appear on DuckDuckGo at all. Bing Places feeds DuckDuckGo maps, Microsoft Edge suggestions, and Cortana. Apple Business Connect (below) feeds DuckDuckGo's local pack, Safari Maps, Apple Maps, and Siri. Both are free and take roughly 20 minutes each.

Go to bingplaces.com and sign in with a Microsoft account (or create one free)
Search for "Zsu Therapy" — if it doesn't exist, click "Add new business"
Enter: Business name — Zsu Therapy · Address — Renaissance Yoga & Wellbeing, Mantle Road, Brockley, London SE4 2EW · Category — Massage Therapist
Add phone number, website (zsutherapy.com), and opening hours
Verify via postcard or phone — allow 1–2 weeks to appear in results
Quick win

Claim Apple Business Connect — 20 minutes, free

Apple Business Connect controls how Zsu appears in Apple Maps, Safari, Siri, and the DuckDuckGo local pack on iOS. A surprisingly large share of SE London's demographic uses iPhones as their primary search device.

Go to businessconnect.apple.com and sign in with an Apple ID
Search for the business — if absent, select "Add your business"
Use exactly the same NAP (Name, Address, Phone) as the Google Business Profile — consistency matters
Add category: Massage Therapist and sub-category Reflexology if available
Upload at least 3 photos — Apple Maps surfaces images prominently
Verify via SMS to the business phone number
Quick win

Fix or remove the empty Package Deals page

The Package Deals page is indexed by Google and currently shows "No plans available" — no content whatsoever. An indexed page with no content is a negative signal. Either:

  • Option A (preferred): Add real content — a "course of 5" deal, or even just a paragraph explaining Zsu offers discounts for booking blocks. Text alone is enough.
  • Option B: In Wix, go to Settings → SEO → Package Deals page → Advanced SEO → set "noindex" so Google stops crawling it.
Quick win

Create a Bark.com profile — for the backlink, not the leads

Zsu has no profile on Bark. The case for creating one is not lead generation — at 15 years trading with 163 five-star reviews, Zsu almost certainly doesn't need to pay to find clients. The case is purely structural: Bark has strong domain authority, its local massage therapist pages rank well in Google, and a listed profile generates a free backlink to zsutherapy.com. That alone is worth the 15 minutes it takes to set up.

Bark's lead credit system — where providers pay £1.50–£3 to respond to each enquiry — is aimed squarely at businesses actively building a new client base. That's not Zsu's situation. Create the free profile, ignore the upsell to buy credits.

Go to bark.com/en/gb/massage-therapy/ and click "Join as a professional"
Create a profile: business name — Zsu Therapy · location — Brockley, SE4 · categories — Massage Therapy, Reflexology, Aromatherapy Massage
Add a profile description that includes "Brockley", "SE4", and 3–4 treatment types — this text is indexed by Google
Link to zsutherapy.com — this is the primary purpose of the listing
Decline any prompts to purchase credits or a paid plan

Medium term

Add a location paragraph to the homepage

The homepage body text contains almost no local keywords. A short paragraph — which can sit discreetly near the footer — gives Google geographic context and will directly improve ranking for "massage Brockley", "massage SE4", and related searches. This is copy Zsu can paste straight into Wix:

SUGGESTED COPY — paste into homepage body
+Based at Renaissance Yoga & Wellbeing on Mantle Road in Brockley (SE4), Zsu Therapy has been serving clients across South East London since 2010. The clinic is a short walk from Brockley Overground station and is easily reached from New Cross, Lewisham, Herne Hill, Forest Hill, Crofton Park, and East Dulwich. Appointments are available Tuesday to Sunday; last-minute slots can be requested by text.

This paragraph works hard for local SEO: it names the postcode, borough, station, and six surrounding neighbourhoods — all terms people search for.

Medium term

Register on Treatwell

Treatwell is the highest-traffic booking platform for massage in London and generates significant inbound referrals. It also creates a strong backlink to zsutherapy.com from a high-authority domain. The listing is free; Treatwell takes a commission only on bookings made through the platform. Competitors including CORE Dulwich, Pure Synergie, Even Thai Spa, Rush&Ry, and BeautyPac all use it — it's the dominant discovery surface in this market.

Note on commission Treatwell's commission is typically 20–30% on platform-originated bookings. Clients who discover Zsu via Treatwell and then rebook directly bypass this entirely — so the net cost over time is lower than it appears.
Medium term

Optimise Google Business Profile categories and photos

The Google Business Profile is listed but likely underoptimised. The following changes are free and take under an hour:

  • Set primary category to Massage Therapist (not just "Therapist" or "Spa")
  • Add secondary categories: Reflexologist, Aromatherapy Service, Sports Massage Therapist
  • Upload at least 10 photos — interior of treatment room, exterior of building/entrance, any certificates
  • Add all services explicitly in the Services section with descriptions and prices
  • Enable booking button linked to zsutherapy.com/book-online
  • Add a business description that includes "Brockley", "SE4", and 3–4 treatment types
  • Respond to all existing Google reviews (even just "Thank you, [name]") — Google weights response rate

Priority 3

Strategic — ongoing

Strategic

Build area landing pages for neighbouring postcodes

Zsu's reviews mention clients travelling from New Cross, Lewisham, Herne Hill, Forest Hill, and East Dulwich. Each of those is a search term ("massage New Cross", "massage Lewisham SE13") that currently has no dedicated page on zsutherapy.com to rank for it.

On Wix, each of these can be a simple page — 200 words, local area mention, link to book. The page title does most of the work:

EXAMPLE — New Cross landing page title & H1
+Title: "Massage Therapist New Cross SE14 | Zsu Therapy Brockley"
+H1: "Massage Therapy Near New Cross, SE14"
+Body: [100–200 words about the area, distance from Brockley, treatments available, book link]

Suggested pages: New Cross SE14 · Lewisham SE13 · Herne Hill SE24 · Forest Hill SE23 · Crofton Park SE4 · East Dulwich SE22.

Strategic

Start a minimal blog — 4 posts per year is enough

Zsu doesn't need to become a content marketer. But 4 posts a year — each targeting a single long-tail search term — would meaningfully expand her keyword footprint and generate backlinks over time.

Good targets for Brockley/SE London:

  • "Deep tissue vs Swedish massage — which is right for you?" — high-volume informational, easy to rank locally
  • "Pregnancy massage in South East London — what to expect" — long-tail, almost no competition
  • "Thai yoga massage Brockley — what makes it different" — very low competition, unique to Zsu's offer
  • "How often should you get a massage?" — Zsu's FAQ page partially addresses this — a proper post would rank for it

Each post: 400–600 words, one internal link back to the relevant treatment page, one mention of Brockley or SE4.
