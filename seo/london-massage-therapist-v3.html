Tips

How to Save & What to Watch For

Book via Treatwell or Fresha Regular flash discounts of 20–50% on established venues. Kannika Thai Spa, CORE Dulwich and Rush&Ry all appear on Fresha with live availability.
Watch the session length Shoreditch Spa uses 55/85-minute sessions rather than 60/90. MASAJ offers 75-minute sessions as a midpoint at £135 — useful if 90 min feels too long.
Loyalty / course bundles Shoreditch Spa: buy 5, get 6th free. Kannika: same. CORE Dulwich: 20% off currently applied. MASAJ Shoreditch: bundle discounts visible on Fresha (save up to 10%).
SW vs SE gap For an equivalent deep tissue massage, SE London (£71–£85 at CORE/Even Thai) costs roughly half the Four Sides London price (£135). Travelling from Battersea to Kennington takes 12 minutes.
Mobile massage Urban starts at £74 for 60 min deep tissue, covering all SE and East London postcodes. Blys starts at £68 in Clapham. Add £10–25 for travel. Wecasa advertises from £22.40 in Peckham (budget/platform).
Hotel luxury tier Four Seasons Tower Bridge offers free spa facility access with any 90-minute+ treatment — factoring this in, the effective per-hour cost vs. standalone spa entry narrows considerably for longer sessions.
